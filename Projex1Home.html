What is PROJEXSHARE?

ProjexShare will let you manage your organizations clientele and projects.

ProjexShare is a complete collaboration solution. It provides a collaborative workspace with the ability to customize and add the functionality you need. Add a group calendar, incorporate a newsfeed, and thousands of other available apps. ProjexShare can take your existing new client/project setup process and automate it or enhance it, with a consistent and structured process based our customizable templates. Take your customer service and communication to the next level by sharing information in near real-time. ProjexShare will bring to your organization a standardization, structure and automation to revolutionize the way you communicate.

  • Keep your files local, secure and the soure of truth
  • Share what you want with clients and partners in a secure cloud environment
  • Sync information on your schedule to provide near real-time updates
  • Simple 3 step wizard automates the entire setup process
  • Time-saving way to keep customers, partners and teams on the same page
  • Improved collaboration and customer service
  • Cut-down on email traffic and sending of large files
  • Never wondering if everyone has the latest version
  • Create a standardized and structured document location
