What is PROJEXSHARE?

Keep your files, leave the headaches

ProjexShare will bring to your organization a standardization, structure and automation to revolutionize the way you communicate.

  • Time-saving way to keep customers, partners and teams on the same page
  • Improved collaboration
  • Improved customer service
  • Cut-down on email traffic and sending of large files
  • Never wondering if everyone has the latest version
  • Standardized and structured document location
