How is PROJEXShare different?

ProjexShare is different from other team collaboration tools, project management apps or document sharing tools. ProjexShare can do all those thing, and then some.

  • Saves time by creating structured and consistent project workspaces. By using customizable templates, each workspace can maintain the same information in the same location across projects. Your clients and partners will always know where to look for the same information.
  • Saves resources (disk space). Most cloud based systems take the point of view that all information in the cloud is the source of truth. This means all users must duplicate the information by syncing it to their own computers. ProjexShare takes the point of view that your company�s internal system is the source of truth. Keeping your project information local and centralized. Keeping the speed of access and saving disk space.
  • Control of shared information and documents that are synced to, and from, the cloud workspace. ProjexShare syncs what you choose, to the cloud. Building a workspace for each project to use for collaboration and sharing. Furthermore, you choose frequency in which the syncing occurs.
  • ProjexShare can be customized to match your current workflow and structure. ProjexShares templates can be used as is or customized to match your current workflow and document structure.
  • ProjexShare is not just a document repository, but provides for a complete and secure workspace in which to collaborate and share project information with your clients and partners. Provides for functionality by adding calendars, task management, news feeds, and much more, all in one workspace.
  • Automates the initial project setup process and maintains the syncing of information in near real-time. ProjexShare utilizes a simple, 3 step wizard to completely setup a new project on your local system, build the cloud workspace and assign security and access.

How does PROJEXShare work?

Simply follow the wizard through 3 simple steps and ProjexShare will setup everything. It creates:

  • The local project repository
  • The cloud portal/workspace for client and partner access
  • Syncing between local repository and cloud workspace
  • Assigns access to the cloud portal and notify clients and partners
